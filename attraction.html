Located north of the river of Brahmaputra, Amingoan is one of the distant districts of Assam. It is more of a small town that has been backward in comparison to the neighboring cities and towns. Tourism, hence, recently started here and it developed as a travel destination mostly because it proved to be the best getaway for those who want some quiet time away from the hustle and bustle of cities.
